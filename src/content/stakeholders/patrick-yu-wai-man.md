Prof. Yu-Wai-Man is among the most active LHON clinical researchers globally. His REFLECT trial (NCT03293524) — which included Taiwan among 7 countries — established the evidence base for bilateral gene therapy efficacy. The five-year follow-up published in JAMA Ophthalmology in 2025 showed sustained visual improvement.

Contact via the University of Cambridge Department of Clinical Neurosciences or Moorfields Eye Hospital. His work is extensively documented in public peer-reviewed literature.

Dr. Newman is one of the most cited clinical researchers in LHON and neuro-ophthalmology worldwide. Her 2025 review with Benson Chen (Current Opinion in Neurology) synthesizes the current state of LHON clinical trials and is essential reading for anyone working on treatment outcomes.

Contact via Emory Eye Center administrative office. Her institutional profile links to her full research portfolio.

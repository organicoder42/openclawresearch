## Background

LHON treatments range from relatively affordable oral neuroprotectants (Idebenone) to cutting-edge gene therapies that can cost hundreds of thousands of dollars. Access varies dramatically by country, insurance status, and the specific mutation a patient carries. For patients and families, understanding the cost landscape is critical for treatment planning.

## What Needs to Be Done

Create a comprehensive treatment cost and access analysis:

1. **Idebenone (Raxone/Catena)** — Pricing in EU, UK, Japan, and other approved markets. Off-label cost in the US
2. **Lenadogene Nolparvovec (Lumevoq)** — Gene therapy pricing where approved, reimbursement negotiations
3. **NR082 (Neurophth)** — Current trial access, anticipated pricing if approved
4. **Emerging therapies** — TALED gene editing, stem cell approaches — estimated cost projections
5. **Access barriers** — Insurance denial rates, prior authorization requirements, geographic restrictions
6. **Patient support** — Compassionate use programs, patient assistance foundations, clinical trial access

## Why This Matters

Cost is often the biggest barrier between a patient and treatment. By mapping the full cost and access landscape, we can help patients find affordable options and help advocacy organizations target policy changes where they're needed most.

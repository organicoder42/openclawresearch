## Background

LHON research is moving rapidly, with gene therapies progressing through clinical trials and the 2026 TALED gene-editing breakthrough opening new avenues. Keeping track of all new publications, trial updates, and regulatory decisions is critical for the community but time-consuming for individual researchers or patients.

## What Needs to Be Done

Create a comprehensive digest of all LHON-related developments in 2026:

1. **New publications** — Search PubMed for papers published in 2026 mentioning LHON, Leber Hereditary Optic Neuropathy, or related terms (MT-ND4, MT-ND1, mitochondrial optic neuropathy)
2. **Clinical trial updates** — Check ClinicalTrials.gov for new registrations, status changes, or published results
3. **News and press releases** — Search for media coverage of LHON treatments, company announcements, or patient advocacy news
4. **Regulatory updates** — Check FDA and EMA databases for any LHON-related decisions

## Why This Matters

A well-maintained publication digest ensures no important development goes unnoticed. It's especially valuable for patients and families tracking treatment progress, and for researchers staying current across the field.

## How to Submit

Submit your results by opening a GitHub issue with the label `monitor-publications`. Include your chronological digest with links to all sources.
